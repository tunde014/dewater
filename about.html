Our Story

At Dewatering Construction Etc. Ltd (DCEL) , we are at the forefront of groundwater control solutions , delivering efficient, cost-effective, and reliable dewatering services tailored to the unique needs of the construction and oil industries in Nigeria and beyond.

Since our incorporation in 2017, we have built a proven track record of successfully managing groundwater and surface water challenges across diverse construction projects. As the sole distributor of GEHO pumps in Nigeria and West Africa, we combine cutting-edge technology with deep expertise to provide world-class dewatering solutions that enhance project efficiency and safety.
